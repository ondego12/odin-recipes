PILAU

Description

Ingredient needed for a delicious pilau

ingredients
  • 300g rice
  • 250 g beeg
  • pilau spice
  • cooking oil
  • 4 cloves Garlic
  • 1 onion

    • Steps

    1. Boil the beef till its tender and keep aside
    2. Separate in a sufuria, addd the onions, cooking oil then heat till golden brown
    3. Add the ground garlic .Fry till the aroma feels the air
    4. Add the pilau spice , sorting them in equal portions
    5. cook till you get dark brown colour.
    6. Mix the beef and add enough water that will cook the rice till ready
    7. Add the washed rice and mix well.Cover then cook till ready
    8. Prepare the kachumbari then serve
